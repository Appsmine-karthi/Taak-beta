About Us

Taak app website

Taak is a community platform built for the skilled workforce. Skilled labour is a critical part of any economy, and a community of skilled workers can be a valuable resource for sharing knowledge, experiences, and best practices. A community can also provide support and resources for workers to continue learning and growing in their fields, and can help connect workers with potential job opportunities. Additionally, a community of skilled workers can advocate for the needs and interests of workers, and can help ensure that the contributions of skilled labour are recognized and valued. With this aim in mind, Taak is built and dedicated to the skilled workforce.

Taak is a product of BlueKites, a company focusing primarily on building digital communities and enriching the relationship between the communities. With more than 40+ years of combined experience in building software products and scaling and managing the large scale operations, the Taak leadership team has strong capabilities to build and deliver digital products which are more meaningful to the common lives.

Our team comprises enthusiastic young professionals who are passionate about building digital products which can impact our society positively. We involved common people who are the end users to seek the feedback in a timely manner. This helped us to improve the overall user experience. Our Marketing team comprises strategic partners who are known in the industry.

With very strong teams across the horizon we are confident to create a positive impact!
